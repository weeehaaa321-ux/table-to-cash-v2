Neom Resort Platform · Expansion Plan

From a single-venue café to a multi-venue resort operating system

One connected platform where every staff member, every guest, every venue, every minute lives on the same surface — built specifically for Red Sea resorts, not retrofitted from a generic PMS.

0.Why this expansion makes sense

The café app already proved the pattern. A resort is the same primitives applied at higher resolution — and the hardest 70% of the work is already shipped.

1

The hard part is shipped

Real-time order routing, role-gated screens, push, multi-round payments, smart upsell, audit + reconciliation — running in production. A resort isn't 10× the complexity; it's the same primitives applied to more contexts.

2

One software, every department

Resorts run a PMS plus 5 disconnected systems for F&B / spa / housekeeping / activities / loyalty. Most operational pain comes from the gaps between them — a guest's allergy entered at check-in invisible to the chef, the spa, the kids club. Unified means it just isn't.

3

Mobile-first guest model

PMSes treat guest's phone as a stretch goal. Mine treats it as the primary remote. Pre-arrival, in-stay, post-departure — one continuous thread, no app install required, just a QR scan.

2.The 12 role-specific surfaces

Each role gets a screen designed for what they actually do all day. A screen is good when the user can complete their most common task in one tap, the second most common in two.

01

Guest phone — universal remote

Lands by QR scan or pre-arrival deep link. Stay summary, current bill, room key, resort map, every venue's menu, every resource bookable, concierge chat. 4+ languages.

02

Front desk

Today's arrivals + departures + in-house. VIP flags, allergies, anniversaries, room ready / not ready. One-tap check-in. Profile in 1 tap — last 5 stays, comp history, preferences.

03

Concierge inbox

Single feed of every request from every channel (in-app, room phone, walk-up, WhatsApp). Status, assignee, snooze. Auto-routes by type. VIPs jump the queue.

04

Housekeeping board

Every room as a tile. Color = status. Real-time sync with front desk. DND-too-long alert to GM. Maintenance tickets raised inline.

05

Engineering

Ticket queue with photo, location, severity, blocked rooms. Closes the loop on housekeeping flags automatically.

06

F&B floor (per venue)

Already shipped. Now scoped per venue — pool deck has one floor manager, fine dining another, lobby bar a third. Owner sees them aggregated.

07

Stations: kitchen / bar / spa / dive

Each station's prep/queue board. Already shipped for KITCHEN / BAR; same model extends to SPA (therapist's appointment list), DIVE (today's groups), ACTIVITY hand-back queue.

08

Cashier / Folio settlement

Walk-in: existing café cashier. In-house: folio view across the stay, allows split (room to corporate card, F&B to personal), receipt per chosen split.

09

Manager-on-Duty (MOD)

The "everything" view. VIPs in-house. Active alerts. Any guest issue in last 24h. RevPAR vs forecast. Override authority for comps and discounts.

10

Owner / GM dashboard

Today: occupancy, ADR, RevPAR, F&B PoR, forward bookings, on-shift staff, top-VIP-in-house. Drill-down: any number → its source events. Live anomaly alerts.

11

Pre-arrival channel

7 days out: WhatsApp / email / SMS in guest's language with personalised offers — room upgrade bid, pre-book activities, anniversary surprise. 24h: digital check-in. Day-of: ETA.

12

Group / event manager

Block bookings (weddings, conferences, retreats). Shared billing (master account + folio splits). Group schedule. Roomming list import from spreadsheet.

3.Eight revenue & cost levers

Each is a published industry benchmark for unified operating platforms — applied to a 100-room Red Sea resort doing roughly 50M EGP/year today.

3.1 — Pre-arrival

Room upgrade bidding

+12M EGP / yr

"Bid for an upgrade to a Suite." Industry: 8–12% of bookings. ~75K EGP/night in pure upgrade revenue from rooms otherwise sitting empty.

3.2 — In-stay

F&B per occupied room

+5M EGP / yr

Industry baseline 600–900 EGP PoR; best-in-class 1,400+. Lift from in-room dining + cross-venue suggestions + resort-wide loyalty. Realistic +200 EGP × 70 rooms × 30 days.

3.3 — Capture

Activities (currently lost cash)

+3M EGP / yr

Resorts lose 30–60% of activity revenue to informal cash. Dive masters, spa walk-ins, kayak rentals on the beach. Captured into folios = real revenue.

3.4 — Highest-margin venue

Spa utilization

+3M EGP / yr

Spa is 60–80% gross. Bottleneck is empty slots, not demand. Real-time visibility + in-app booking + flash-deal push lifts utilization 15–25%.

3.5 — Loss prevention

Comp shrinkage retained

+1M EGP / yr

Industry shrinkage: 1.5–3% of revenue on comps and unjustified discounts. Audit trail + variance flags in real time = closed.

3.6 — Cost

Labor productivity

+3.5M EGP saved / yr

F&B captain covers 2× tables. Concierge handles 4× tickets. Housekeeping shaves 8 min/room. 15–20% labor cost reduction on a 60-staff op.

3.7 — Repeat

Loyalty repeat-rate

+0.5M EGP / yr

Persistent profile + post-stay offer = +5–8 percentage points repeat-rate. Repeat guests are 3–5× cheaper to acquire than new ones.

3.8 — Reputation

Review-driven ADR uplift

+1.5M EGP / yr

Real-time bad-experience signals → MOD intervention before checkout. Pre-emptive recovery turns 2-stars into 4-stars 70% of the time. 2–5% ADR uplift next quarter.

4.Implementation phases

Each phase ships independently and adds revenue / ops value before the next one starts. There is never a "big bang" go-live waiting on every feature.

Shipped · in production
today

Phase 0 — Café-as-Venue

  • Single-venue F&B + activities + cashier + smart upsell.
  • This is the foundation. A resort is N of these, plus rooms, plus the cross-venue glue.
Phase 1 · Resort core
4 – 6 weeks

Minimum viable resort

  • New schema: Property, Room, Booking, Stay, Folio, Charge, Guest (promoted from VipGuest), Plan.
  • Front desk (check-in / check-out, room assignment).
  • Folio (per stay, charge-to-room from any venue).
  • Owner dashboard with rooms occupancy / RevPAR / ADR.
  • Pre-arrival WhatsApp / email channel (basic).
  • Owner can start replacing their PMS on day 1 of phase 1.
Phase 2 · Venue network
3 – 4 weeks

Multi-venue inside one property

  • Lobby bar, pool café, fine dining, room service all distinct Venues.
  • Cross-venue cart: order breakfast at the pool while dinner reservation at the restaurant is upcoming.
  • Per-venue floor managers, aggregated under a single property MOD.
  • Loyalty: charges across venues count toward one tier.
Phase 3 · Experience layer
6 – 8 weeks

Concierge, housekeeping, spa, inventory

  • Concierge inbox with multi-channel ingest.
  • Housekeeping board with real-time room status.
  • Engineering tickets with photo + closure loop.
  • Appointment / Resource booking (spa, dive, cabana, dinner reservation) with slot calendars.
  • Inventory: minibar par + restock workflow.
  • VIP profile depth: anniversaries, allergies surface across venues, persistent across stays.
Phase 4 · Intelligence layer
ongoing

AI butler, PMS sync, predictive ops

  • AI butler: guest texts in natural language, system understands intent (book / order / request / question), answers in their language, escalates to human when unsure.
  • PMS integration: Booking.com / Expedia / direct site sync.
  • Pre-arrival upsell evolved: dynamic upgrade pricing based on current occupancy, weather-aware push.
  • Predictive ops: forecast tomorrow's covers per venue from arrivals + check-out + day-pass sales.
  • Voice-of-guest analytics: NLP over reviews + concierge messages surfacing systematic issues weeks before they hit TripAdvisor.

7.Engagement model

Option A

Strategic build

Lump-sum implementation fee for resort core + venue network + experience layer. Year-1 ops cost capped. Year-2+ flat platform fee. Source code escrow available.

Option B · most aligned

Revenue share

No upfront fee. I deliver the platform. I take a single-digit % of incremental revenue the platform demonstrably moves vs a 60-day baseline. Caps after 24 months. I only earn if the uplift is real.

Option C

Equity stake

For an investor-owner who sees this as a category. I contribute the platform + ongoing development as founding tech equity. We jointly take it to other resorts in the region.

8.The pitch — first meeting, one paragraph

Today your resort runs on five disconnected systems and a lot of WhatsApp. The PMS at the front desk doesn't talk to the F&B register, which doesn't talk to the spa book, which doesn't talk to housekeeping. Every gap between those systems is where revenue leaks and guest experience fragments.

I've already built a unified platform that's running live in a Dahab café — every staff member, every guest, every venue on one screen, in real time, in Arabic and English. The same primitives scale to your resort: multi-day stays, charge-to-room folios, multi-venue F&B, spa appointments, housekeeping, concierge inbox, smart pre-arrival upsell.

On a 100-room resort, conservatively, this lifts gross revenue ~30M EGP/year while saving ~15% labor cost. Not a 12-month implementation — phase 1 ships in 4–6 weeks, you replace one system at a time, and at no point is your resort offline.

Worst case: you have a better F&B system inside two weeks. Best case: in six months you have the most modern resort operations stack on the Red Sea, built for you, not retrofitted from a generic product.
